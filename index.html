Office Block

Modelled using IES VE.

Tools: IES VE

Primary School Building:Modelled to comply with Part L with Natural Ventilation

Role:Created 3D Model. Assigned low U-value building fabric elements, lighting and ASHP (COP=3.5).Benchmarked TGD-030 ensured atleast 8 l/s/pupil, < 1000ppm CO2 & Dry bulb temp. not exceeding 25oC for > 5% of the year. Simulated based off Dublin89 Weather database

Results:Building met Part L Regs with EPC=0.5, CPC=0.64, RER=0.44, Final BER=A2.Range Test: Dry bulb temp.exceeding 25oC= 23 to 45 hours, CO2 never exceeded 1000ppm

Tools: IES VE, AutoCAD
